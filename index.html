Box Turtle

The captivating box turtle is a type of reptile that belongs to a group of marsh turtles. Known for dwelling in woodlands, brushy grasslands and areas near the streams and ponds, these friendly creatures often occupy territory of 750 feet in diameter.

  • Scientific Name: Terrapene
  • Average Length: 8 Inhes
  • Average Lifespan: 50 years
  • Hibernation: Mid-September to mid-October
